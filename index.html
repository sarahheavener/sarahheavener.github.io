Child Mind Institue

As many as 1 in 5 children struggle with mental health or learning disorders. Child Mind Institute is a nonprofit organization dedicated to transforming children's lives who suffer from these conditions. They help children and their families by providing effective care, spreading educational resources, training educators in neglected communities, and developing new treatments. These children are the future of the world, you can help them today by donating or spreading the word.
